Trek Overview

The Annapurna Circuit Trek is a world-renowned adventure that circles the Annapurna Massif, offering a stunning variety of landscapes from lush subtropical forests to the arid highlands of the Manang and Mustang regions. Highlights include crossing the Thorong La Pass (5,416m), visiting the sacred Muktinath Temple, and experiencing the rich Gurung and Thakali cultures.

Highlights

  • Panoramic views of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, and Machhapuchhre
  • Cross the challenging Thorong La Pass (5,416m)
  • Visit Muktinath Temple, sacred to Hindus and Buddhists
  • Explore traditional Gurung and Thakali villages

Detailed Itinerary (14 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport, transfer to your hotel, and attend a trek briefing in the evening.

Day 2: Drive/Fly to Pokhara (820m)

Take a scenic 6-7 hour drive or 30-minute flight to Pokhara, enjoying views of the Himalayas and lakes.

Day 3: Drive to Besisahar (760m) & Trek to Bhulbhule (840m)

Drive to Besisahar (3-4 hours) and trek 2-3 hours to Bhulbhule through lush greenery.

Day 4: Trek to Jagat (1,300m)

Trek 5-6 hours through rice fields and small villages along the Marsyangdi River.

Day 5: Trek to Dharapani (1,860m)

Continue 5-6 hours through forests and cross suspension bridges to Dharapani.

Day 6: Trek to Chame (2,670m)

Trek 5-6 hours with views of Annapurna II and Lamjung Himal, reaching Chame.

Day 7: Trek to Pisang (3,200m)

Trek 5-6 hours through pine forests and past apple orchards to Pisang.

Day 8: Trek to Manang (3,540m)

Trek 4-5 hours to Manang, with stunning views of Annapurna and Gangapurna.

Day 9: Acclimatization Day in Manang

Rest and hike to nearby viewpoints or monasteries to acclimatize to the altitude.

Day 10: Trek to Yak Kharka (4,050m)

Trek 3-4 hours through high alpine meadows to Yak Kharka.

Day 11: Trek to Thorong Phedi (4,450m)

Trek 3-4 hours to Thorong Phedi, preparing for the pass crossing.

Day 12: Trek to Muktinath (3,760m) via Thorong La Pass (5,416m)

Early start for a challenging 8-10 hour trek over Thorong La Pass to Muktinath.

Day 13: Trek to Jomsom (2,720m)

Trek 5-6 hours to Jomsom, passing through the arid Mustang region.

Day 14: Fly to Pokhara and Drive/Fly to Kathmandu

Morning flight to Pokhara, then drive or fly back to Kathmandu. Farewell dinner in the evening.
