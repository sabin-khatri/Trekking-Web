Our Story

Our Journey, Our Passion, Our Promise

Our Mission

We believe trekking is more than just a walk; it’s a journey into the heart of nature, culture, and oneself. Nepal Treks was born from a deep-rooted passion for the Himalayas and a desire to share its magic with the world.

Our mission is to provide authentic, safe, and sustainable trekking experiences that forge a lasting connection between our guests and the majestic landscapes and warm-hearted people of Nepal.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Nepal Treks

2010: Founded

Nepal Treks was born from Dorje Sherpa's vision to share the Himalayas with the world.

2015: First International Group

Led our first international trekking group to Everest Base Camp, a major milestone.

2018: Sustainability Pledge

Committed to eco-friendly practices, partnering with local communities for sustainable tourism.

2023: 1000th Trekker

Celebrated guiding our 1000th trekker, a testament to our growing family of adventurers.
