Trek Overview

The Langtang Valley Trek, often called "the valley of glaciers," offers a serene and less-crowded trekking experience in the Langtang Region of Nepal. This trek takes you through lush forests, traditional Tamang villages, and up to Kyanjin Gompa, where you can enjoy breathtaking views of Langtang Lirung (7,227m) and surrounding glaciers. The trail is rich in biodiversity, passing through Langtang National Park, and provides a deep cultural immersion with the Tamang community.

Highlights

  • Stunning views of Langtang Lirung and other Himalayan peaks
  • Visit Kyanjin Gompa, a historic Buddhist monastery
  • Experience authentic Tamang culture and hospitality

Detailed Itinerary (8 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport, transfer to your hotel, and attend a trek briefing in the evening.

Day 2: Drive to Syabrubesi (1,550m)

Take a scenic 7-8 hour drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi, the starting point of the trek.

Day 3: Trek to Lama Hotel (2,470m)

Trek 5-6 hours through forests and along the Langtang River to Lama Hotel.

Day 4: Trek to Mundu (3,543m)

Trek 5-6 hours through rhododendron forests and Tamang villages to Mundu.

Day 5: Trek to Kyanjin Gompa (3,870m)

Trek 3-4 hours to Kyanjin Gompa, with stunning views of Langtang Lirung.

Day 6: Acclimatization Day at Kyanjin Gompa

Explore Kyanjin Gompa, visit the monastery, or hike to Kyanjin Ri (4,773m) for panoramic views.

Day 7: Trek back to Lama Hotel (2,470m)

Trek 5-6 hours back to Lama Hotel, retracing the route through the valley.

Day 8: Trek to Syabrubesi and Drive to Kathmandu

Trek 4-5 hours to Syabrubesi, then drive 7-8 hours back to Kathmandu. Farewell dinner in the evening.
