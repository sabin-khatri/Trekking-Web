Trek Overview

The Poon Hill Trek is a short and accessible trek in the Annapurna Region, perfect for beginners or those with limited time. Famous for its stunning sunrise views from Poon Hill (3,210m), this trek offers breathtaking panoramas of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, and Machapuchare. The trail winds through charming Gurung and Magar villages like Ghandruk and Ghorepani, and vibrant rhododendron forests, especially beautiful in spring. This moderate trek is ideal for families, beginners, or anyone seeking a quick Himalayan adventure.

Highlights

  • Breathtaking sunrise views from Poon Hill over Annapurna and Dhaulagiri
  • Explore Gurung and Magar villages like Ghandruk and Ghorepani
  • Trek through vibrant rhododendron forests
  • Panoramic views of Annapurna South, Machapuchare, and more

Detailed Itinerary (5 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m) & Drive to Pokhara (820m)

Arrive in Kathmandu, transfer to Pokhara (6-7 hour drive), and attend a trek briefing.

Day 2: Drive to Nayapul (1,070m) & Trek to Tikhedhunga (1,540m)

Drive 1.5 hours to Nayapul, then trek 3-4 hours to Tikhedhunga through lush forests.

Day 3: Trek to Ghorepani (2,860m)

Trek 5-6 hours to Ghorepani through rhododendron forests, arriving at the Magar village.

Day 4: Poon Hill Sunrise (3,210m) & Trek to Tadapani (2,630m)

Hike early to Poon Hill for sunrise views, then trek 4-5 hours to Tadapani.

Day 5: Trek to Ghandruk (1,940m) & Drive to Pokhara

Trek 3-4 hours to Ghandruk, explore the Gurung village, then drive 2 hours back to Pokhara.
