Trek Overview

The Manaslu Circuit Trek is a remote and less-crowded alternative to other Himalayan treks, circling the majestic Mount Manaslu (8,163m), the world‚Äôs eighth-highest peak. This challenging trek takes you through diverse landscapes, from subtropical forests to high-altitude alpine meadows, and crosses the dramatic Larkya La Pass (5,160m). Along the way, explore the rich Tibetan-influenced culture of the Tsum Valley and Samagaon, with ancient monasteries and vibrant local traditions.

Highlights

  • Stunning views of Mount Manaslu and surrounding peaks
  • Cross the challenging Larkya La Pass (5,160m)
  • Explore Tibetan Buddhist culture in Samagaon and Tsum Valley
  • Trek through diverse landscapes and remote villages

Detailed Itinerary (15 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport, transfer to your hotel, and attend a trek briefing in the evening.

Day 2: Drive to Soti Khola (710m)

Drive 7-8 hours from Kathmandu to Soti Khola via Arughat, the trek‚Äôs starting point.

Day 3: Trek to Machha Khola (870m)

Trek 6-7 hours along the Budhi Gandaki River through subtropical forests.

Day 4: Trek to Jagat (1,340m)

Trek 6-7 hours through narrow trails and small villages to Jagat.

Day 5: Trek to Deng (1,860m)

Trek 6-7 hours, entering the Manaslu Conservation Area with views of Shringi Himal.

Day 6: Trek to Namrung (2,630m)

Trek 6-7 hours through forests and Tibetan-influenced villages.

Day 7: Trek to Samagaon (3,520m)

Trek 6-7 hours to Samagaon, with stunning views of Manaslu.

Day 8: Acclimatization Day in Samagaon

Rest and explore Samagaon, with optional hikes to Manaslu Base Camp or a local monastery.

Day 9: Trek to Samdo (3,860m)

Trek 4-5 hours to the remote village of Samdo near the Tibetan border.

Day 10: Acclimatization Day in Samdo

Rest and explore Samdo, with optional hikes to nearby viewpoints.

Day 11: Trek to Dharmasala (4,480m)

Trek 4-5 hours to Dharmasala, preparing for the Larkya La Pass.

Day 12: Trek to Bimthang (3,720m) via Larkya La Pass (5,160m)

Cross the Larkya La Pass (8-9 hours) with panoramic views, descending to Bimthang.

Day 13: Trek to Tilije (2,300m)

Trek 5-6 hours through forests and villages to Tilije.

Day 14: Trek to Tal (1,700m)

Trek 5-6 hours along the Dudh Khola to Tal.

Day 15: Drive to Kathmandu

Drive 8-9 hours back to Kathmandu, with a farewell dinner in the evening.
