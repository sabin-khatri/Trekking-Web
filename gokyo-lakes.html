Trek Overview

The Gokyo Lakes Trek is a spectacular alternative to the Everest Base Camp trek, offering breathtaking views of four 8,000m peaks (Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu) and the serene turquoise Gokyo Lakes. This trek takes you through the heart of the Khumbu Region, passing vibrant Sherpa villages, the bustling market of Namche Bazaar, and the majestic Ngozumpa Glacier. A highlight is climbing Gokyo Ri (5,357m) for unparalleled Himalayan vistas.

Highlights

  • Panoramic views of Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu from Gokyo Ri
  • Explore the turquoise Gokyo Lakes and Ngozumpa Glacier
  • Experience Sherpa culture in Namche Bazaar and Gokyo
  • Trek through the scenic Sagarmatha National Park

Detailed Itinerary (13 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport, transfer to your hotel, and attend a trek briefing in the evening.

Day 2: Fly to Lukla (2,860m) & Trek to Phakding (2,610m)

Take a 35-minute flight to Lukla, then trek 3-4 hours to Phakding along the Dudh Koshi River.

Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Trek 5-6 hours through pine forests and suspension bridges to the Sherpa hub of Namche Bazaar.

Day 4: Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

Rest and explore Namche Bazaar, with optional hikes to Everest View Hotel for panoramic views.

Day 5: Trek to Phortse Thanga (3,680m)

Trek 5-6 hours through rhododendron forests and along the Dudh Koshi to Phortse Thanga.

Day 6: Trek to Machhermo (4,470m)

Trek 4-5 hours through yak pastures and small villages to Machhermo.

Day 7: Trek to Gokyo (4,790m)

Trek 4-5 hours to Gokyo, passing the first two Gokyo Lakes and the Ngozumpa Glacier.

Day 8: Acclimatization Day in Gokyo

Explore the Gokyo Lakes or hike to the fifth lake for views of Cho Oyu and the Ngozumpa Glacier.

Day 9: Climb Gokyo Ri (5,357m) & Trek to Thagnak (4,700m)

Early morning climb to Gokyo Ri for sunrise views, then trek 3-4 hours to Thagnak.

Day 10: Trek to Dzongla (4,830m) via Cho La Pass (5,420m)

Cross the challenging Cho La Pass (7-8 hours) to Dzongla, with stunning glacier views.

Day 11: Trek to Lobuche (4,940m)

Trek 4-5 hours to Lobuche, joining the Everest Base Camp trail.

Day 12: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Trek 6-7 hours back to Namche Bazaar through scenic trails.

Day 13: Trek to Lukla (2,860m)

Trek 6-7 hours to Lukla, completing the trekking loop.

Day 14: Fly to Kathmandu

Morning flight to Kathmandu, followed by a farewell dinner in the evening.
