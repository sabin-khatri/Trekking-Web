Trek Overview

The Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the world's most famous trekking adventures, taking you through the heart of the Khumbu region to the foot of the world's highest mountain. This challenging yet incredibly rewarding journey offers unparalleled views of Himalayan giants, insight into Sherpa culture, and the satisfaction of reaching the base camp of Mount Everest.

Highlights

  • Spectacular views of Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, and Ama Dablam
  • Explore Namche Bazaar, the gateway to the high Himalayas
  • Visit Tengboche Monastery, the spiritual center of the region
  • Experience Sherpa culture and visit traditional villages

Detailed Itinerary (14 Days)

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport where you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Briefing about the trek in the evening.

Day 2: Fly to Lukla (2,840m) & trek to Phakding (2,610m)

Spectacular 35-minute flight to Lukla, then an easy 3-4 hour trek through Sherpa villages to Phakding.

Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Cross suspension bridges over the Dudh Koshi River and enter Sagarmatha National Park. First glimpse of Everest on a clear day.

Day 4: Acclimatization day in Namche Bazaar

Hike to Everest View Hotel for panoramic views of Everest, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam. Visit the Sherpa Museum and local market.

Day 5: Trek to Tengboche (3,860m)

Gradual climb through rhododendron forests to Tengboche, home to the largest monastery in the Khumbu region.

Day 6: Trek to Dingboche (4,410m)

Descend through lush forests then climb up to Dingboche, a beautiful village with impressive views of Ama Dablam.

Day 7: Acclimatization day in Dingboche

Hike to Nagarjun Hill for spectacular views of Makalu, Lhotse, and Cho Oyu. Learn about high-altitude adaptation.

Day 8: Trek to Lobuche (4,910m)

Gradual ascent to Lobuche, passing through memorials dedicated to climbers who lost their lives on Everest.

Day 9: Trek to Gorak Shep (5,140m), visit Everest Base Camp (5,364m)

Challenging trek to Gorak Shep, then continue to Everest Base Camp to stand at the foot of the world's highest mountain.

Day 10: Hike to Kala Patthar (5,545m), trek to Pheriche (4,240m)

Early morning hike to Kala Patthar for sunrise views of Everest, then descend to Pheriche.

Day 11: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

Long descent through Pangboche and Tengboche back to Namche Bazaar.

Day 12: Trek to Lukla (2,840m)

Final day of trekking, retracing steps through the Dudh Koshi valley to Lukla.

Day 13: Fly to Kathmandu

Morning flight back to Kathmandu. Free time for exploration and farewell dinner in the evening.

Day 14: Final departure

Transfer to the airport for your final departure, or extend your stay in Nepal.
